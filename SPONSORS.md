## FAQ

### Q: Can I sponsor for just one month?
A: Yes! You can sponsor for any duration. Even one month helps.

### Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: GitHub Sponsors accepts credit/debit cards. Buy Me a Coffee accepts various payment methods.

### Q: Can I get an invoice for my sponsorship?
A: Yes, GitHub provides invoices for all sponsorships.

### Q: Is my sponsorship tax-deductible?
A: Consult with a tax professional. In many jurisdictions, sponsorships of open source projects may be tax-deductible as business expenses.

### Q: Can I cancel my sponsorship?
A: Yes, you can cancel at any time through GitHub.

### Q: What happens if funding goals aren't met?
A: The project will continue, but development may be slower. All core features will remain free and open source.
